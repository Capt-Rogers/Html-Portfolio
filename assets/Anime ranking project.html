My Top animes of All Time

Deathnote

The OG anime for any anime lover.
The artistic style is somewhat old but the story plotting and characters are on another level.

Erased

This anime is about time travel and a mystery in a small town.
It is of 12 episodes and worth binge watching.

Attack on titan

This anime questions about what is it that makes someone bad or good.
It potrays that in this world nothing is bad or good it is just all about survival.
